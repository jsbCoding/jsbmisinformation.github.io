New Discovery on Orion B
Scientists have discovered a new species of Doyicmu Fungi on Orion-b4. They have reported that it displays bioluminescence when exposed to moisture, and can exhibit growth of over 5 feet per day.

Bob Chandler announces updates to IFIE space protocols
Bob Chandler has announced this Friday that the space protocols that have been used since IFIE’s conception are in dire need of an overhaul.
According to research, many of the new Voyages need Sector specific protocol in order to manage the space-jump dysphoria that has become common place for jump levels of over 15%.
While IFIE has not given a specific date for the overhauls, Chandler has assured reporters that the changes will be made in a timely and responsible manner.
This comes just in time for William Right, who had stated his issues with the current protocols, who is currently beginning his final preparations for the Class A Sybil ranger.
