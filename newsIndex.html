New Discovery on Orion B
Scientists have discovered a new species of Doyicmu Fungi on Orion-b4. They have reported that it displays bioluminescence when exposed to moisture, and can exhibit growth of over 5 feet per day.
One of the applications could be the issues of gathering food in Sector 16. If the fungus can be grown in nutrient deficient environments like its locality, there could be positive implications. Micheal Fursor had this to say:
“We are constantly looking for ways to improve the food shortage situation in Sector 16, and are willing to explore this new method to help foster the colonies.”
