The International Foundation for Interstellar Exploration.
Marie Feuler
Marie Feuler is the Assistant Director of space control. At IFIE we only allow the most experienced and dedicated members of their fields to join our board. Feuler has had a long history of service in the Galal space crew, and has dedicated her life to Zu-149 research.
Bob Chandler
Bob Chandler is the current director of space control. Along with Marie Feuler, he manages the vast proportion of Class a to c space cruisers from sectors 14 to 21. He has over 20 years of experience in IFIE, and has shown again and again that his quick thinking and judgement allow him to make the decisions we can stand behind.
Nick Fault
Nick Fault manages supplies and research goals for the management board. He is chief in command of the Orion research commision, and has a certificate in tioblo-graphic isono-resonances.
Alice Horris
Alice Horris is our current chief of research. All of the articles published on the reports board are read by Horris and her team. The current branch handles over 14,000 reports per annum.
William Right
William Right is head mechanical engineer in our Dymaridium branch of manufacturing. He is dedicated to ensuring that only usable and safe products go out the manufactory's doors. Test flights and safety tests are not only a regular occurance, but a neccessity to ensuring that IFIE's government approved equipment remains reliable.
Laura White
Laura White is our Communications Manager. She is responsible for 10 sectors worth of communication between Class C to A spacecraft. Her team is responsible for the high response times we have achieved over the past decade..
